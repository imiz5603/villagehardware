Welcome to Village Hardware

Established for over 30 years, Village Hardware is your 'one stop' general hardware, art and crafts shop.

We are retail suppliers of tools, hardware, paint, supply and program remotes, and carry an extensive range of art supplies, decoupage and craft materials.

Located at Glenwood Village, Moore Road, Durban — with convenient off-road parking right outside.

Knowledgeable and friendly service from Barbara and her Team.

Come in and browse our extensive range of hardware. We don’t believe in pre-packs — buy only what you need.

Services Offered

General Hardware & Tools

From pliers and chisels to screws, ladders, epoxies and more. If you name it, we’ll probably have it!

Paint & Painting Supplies

Plascon paint mixing (from old to latest shades), full range of Woodoc & Hammerite, and all painting tools.

Art & Craft Materials

Brands include Windsor & Newton, Artiste, Chromacryl, Daler Rowney, Copic, Caio, and more. Custom stretched canvases in 3 days.

Plumbing & Electrical

Plumbing spares and electrical items including neon tubes, energy savers and incandescent bulbs.

Remote Controls

Wide range of gate remotes programmed while you wait. Limited TV/PVR remotes — please call to check.

Batteries

Full range of alkaline, acid and rechargeable batteries for alarms, gates, watches, calculators — fitted free!

Crafts

Heritage products, wooden blanks, mosaics, Decralead, pewter, clays, balsa wood, foam board and more. Student lists welcome.

Garden & Cleaning

Garden tools, plant foods, pesticides, cleaning materials, watering cans and more.
